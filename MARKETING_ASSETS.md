## Contact Form Field Specifications

### Form Fields (Required)
1. **Full Name** (Text input, required)
   - Placeholder: "Your name"
   - Validation: Min 2 characters, max 100 characters

2. **Company Name** (Text input, required)
   - Placeholder: "Organization name"
   - Validation: Min 2 characters, max 150 characters

3. **Email Address** (Email input, required)
   - Placeholder: "your@email.com"
   - Validation: Valid email format

4. **Phone Number** (Phone input, optional)
   - Placeholder: "(555) 123-4567"
   - Validation: Valid phone format

5. **Organization Type** (Dropdown, required)
   - Options:
     - Healthcare Provider
     - Professional Services (Law, Accounting, Finance)
     - SaaS / Technology
     - Manufacturing
     - Financial Services
     - Other

6. **Current Security Challenges** (Textarea, required)
   - Placeholder: "Tell us about your security challenges, compliance requirements, or concerns..."
   - Min characters: 20
   - Max characters: 2000

7. **How did you hear about us?** (Dropdown, optional)
   - Options:
     - Search Engine
     - LinkedIn
     - Referral
     - Social Media
     - Other

- **Error Message**: "There was an issue submitting your form. Please try again or contact us directly at adam@ceyrva.com"
